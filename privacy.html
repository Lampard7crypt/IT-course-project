Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Saturday 18th January, 2026
Last Updated: Saturday 7th February, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Car Sales Inc. ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website here and use our services to buy or sell vehicles.

2. Legal Compliance & Governance

We process your personal data in strict adherence to the laws of Kenya and international best practices. This policy is drafted in accordance with:

  • The Data Protection Act (2019): Ensuring your personal data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
  • The Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act (2018): Safeguarding against unauthorized access and ensuring the integrity of our systems.
  • The National ICT Policy (2019): Aligning with national standards for information communication and technology.
  • Official Guidelines from the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC): Following the regulatory framework for data privacy and protection.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification: Name, national ID/passport number, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Vehicle Information: Logbook details, vehicle registration numbers, photos, and maintenance history (for sellers).
  • Financial Data: Payment details, transaction history, and MPESA/bank transaction codes (we do not store complete credit card numbers).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior via cookies.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate the buying and selling of vehicles.
  • To verify the ownership and legal status of vehicles listed on our platform.
  • To process payments and prevent fraud.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account, listings, or inquiries.
  • To comply with legal obligations under the Data Protection Act (2019).

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. In compliance with the Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act (2018), we maintain secure servers and use encryption (SSL) for sensitive data transmission.

6. Your Rights

Under the Data Protection Act (2019), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of false or misleading data.
  • Request the deletion of your data (Right to Erasure).
  • Object to the processing of your data for direct marketing.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at protectoinofficer@gmail.com.

7. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Regulatory Authorities: Example: NTSA (National Transport and Safety Authority) for vehicle verification, or the ODPC if required by law.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business (e.g., payment processors, hosting services), provided they agree to keep this information confidential.
